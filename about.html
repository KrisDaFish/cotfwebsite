Who Are We?

We are a team of passionate high school students dedicated to making computer science accessible and exciting for everyone. Our mission is to inspire the next generation by sharing our love for coding and technology. Through engaging lessons and community outreach, we aim to empower young minds, foster creativity, and help students build a strong foundation in computer science for their future success.
