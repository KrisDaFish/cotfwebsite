Who Are We?

We are a group of passionate high school students committed to making computer science easy, fun, and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to inspire the next generation by sharing our enthusiasm for coding and technology. Through interactive lessons, hands-on activities, and community outreach, we aim to spark creativity, build confidence, and help students develop a strong foundation in computer science to prepare them for a successful future.
